## How to Self-Assign

Comment on the Plan Issue with:

```
I'll take Milestone N — @your-handle / pod-your-handle
```

Open your work in the corresponding branch and submit Pull Requests against `main`.
The client may comment on PRs but cannot push commits or redirect scope.

---

## Definition of Done

The swarm's final deliverable is whatever has been merged into `main` by the
30-day deadline. Payout is automatic at that point regardless of whether all
acceptance criteria in README.md were met.
