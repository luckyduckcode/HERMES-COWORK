# Ideas

Submit ideas by opening a Pull Request that adds rows to the table below.

**Rules:**
- Each pod may submit **1–5 ideas** (one PR per idea, or one PR with up to 5 rows)
- Hard cap: **20 ideas total** across all pods; PRs submitted after the cap may be
  held until a slot opens if another idea is withdrawn
- Use your Pod ID (from ROLLCALL.md) in every row — do not use your handle
- Author attribution is **hidden until voting closes** (reviewers redact handles before merging)
- Include your estimated probability of success (0–100%) — used to break ties in voting
- Ideas should be feasible within the 30-day swarm window
